Files:
------
green_vegetation.csv
  USGS ID: splib07a_Aspen_Aspen-1_green-top_ASDFRa_AREF
  Description: Green aspen leaf (top side), typical green vegetation canopy.
  Instrument: ASD FieldSpec, 1nm sampling, 414-2446 nm (1932 channels).
  Features: Chlorophyll absorption at 450nm and 670nm, green peak at 550nm,
            red edge 680-750nm, NIR plateau ~0.47, SWIR water absorption.

npv_dry_grass.csv
  USGS ID: splib07a_Grass_Golden_Dry_GDS480_ASDFRa_AREF
  Description: Golden dry grass (GDS480), non-photosynthetic vegetation.
  Instrument: ASD FieldSpec, 1nm sampling, 350-2500 nm (2151 channels).
  Features: No chlorophyll absorption, moderate visible reflectance,
            cellulose/lignin absorption features at ~2100nm.

bare_soil_sand.csv
  USGS ID: splib07a_Sand_DWO-3-DEL2a_no_vis.oil_ASDFRa_AREF
  Description: Clean beach sand (Deepwater Horizon reference), bare substrate.
  Instrument: ASD FieldSpec, 1nm sampling, 350-2425 nm (1742 channels).
  Features: Monotonically increasing reflectance from visible to SWIR,
            no vegetation features, quartz absorption near 2200nm.

Format: CSV with columns wavelength_nm (nanometers) and reflectance (0-1).
Nodata values from original SPECPR files have been removed.
